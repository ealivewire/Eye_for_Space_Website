Confirmed Planets
The source of this data is the NASA Exoplanet Archive, an online astronomical exoplanet and stellar catalog and data service that collates and cross-correlates astronomical data and information on exoplanets and their host stars. The archive is dedicated to collecting and serving important public data sets involved in the search for and characterization of extrasolar planets and their host stars. All data in the Exoplanet Archive are vetted by a team of astronomers (team roster) and are linked back to the original literature reference.
(Source: https://exoplanetarchive.ipac.caltech.edu/docs/intro.html)
